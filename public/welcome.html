What is this?

For people tired of IG, Twitter/X, Substack and other mainstream platforms.

Semi-private by design

  • You can opt-in to let your posts appear on the Discover page—or not, and let only people who know your handle find you.
  • Comments are private by default, only you see them. You can publish select comments plus your reply via "Pinned feedback".
  • Per-post visibility: "protected" means only people who are already your subscribers will see that post. New subscribers or people visiting your page directly won't see it.

Features

  • Simple compose page and timeline of your posts
  • Email subscription and weekly digest for readers
  • Reposts to Bluesky and Telegram for more distribution (opt-in)
